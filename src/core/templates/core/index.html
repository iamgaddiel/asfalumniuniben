The Alumni body of ASF UNIBEN - UBTH is a network of professionals around the globe committed to furthering the gospel of Jesus Christ, and fostering a sense of brotherhood and camaraderie through our activities, platforms and events.

Our Membership Community

Membership to the Alumni body is open to all graduates of the University of Benin who were members of Anglican Students Fellowship. We look forward to welcoming fresh graduates, as well as older alumni members whom we may have lost touch with.

We have a vibrant and productive representation both in Nigeria, the African Continent as well as other nations across the world. No matter your location, there's definitely an ASF UNIBEN Alumni community near you!

How You can be Active as an Alumni

Being an Alumni is a perfect opportunity to partake in the glorious mandate of furthering the gospel of Christ on a global level. Your participation ensures that the wonderful times we spent in service during our school days can be relived and better experienced.

On this platform, you can get acquainted with our events, programs and updates; interact with Alumni members in your region and also find out ways we can continue to be a blessing to the student fellowship.

You can also advertise your business and professional services as well as connect with other alumni on the Biz Hub section of this website.
